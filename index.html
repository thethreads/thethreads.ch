Who are we?
The Threads is an a cappella group formed of current and former students of ETH Zurich.

In late 2014, a small group of friends decided that they would like to meet up and sing. A few years later, The Threads have had a chance to entertain family and friends in Zurich, spreading the warm spirit of music with joy and laughter. The Threads sings songs from a large variety of genres including pop, classical and world music.
